About us

Who are we? Hanumant Travel & Tours is a family-owned tour operator and travel agency located in Prayagraj Uttar Pradesh,India. We specialize in tours to different cities of India but are always expanding and enhancing our wide range of offers, as well as adding new and exciting tours within Uttar Pradesh State. A family-owned business based in Prayagraj (Allahabad), Hanumant Travels & Tours is founded by Mr. Rajeev Nangia in 2000 and since that humble beginning it has grown into a trusted resource that has helped tens of thousands of people better enjoy their travel.
